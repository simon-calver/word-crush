Basic

The current tile is displayed at the top of the screen. The next two tiles are to the left of this. Tap or click on any empty slot in the grid to place the current tile. Get points for spelling words! Words can be either across or down. Your score is displayed in the top right. Longer words score more points.

The game ends when you have used all 90 tiles or when no more tiles can be placed.

Advanced

Spell multiple words with a single tile to get extra points. Double tap a tile when placing to lock it, tap again to unlock. Valid words including a locked tile will not be counted until it is unlocked.

Get bonus points for using all tiles or for clearing the grid.
